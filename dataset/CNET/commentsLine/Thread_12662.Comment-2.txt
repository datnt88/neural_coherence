enter the routers configuration via your web browser, and make your security adjustments.
the manual will have the lowdown, but some of the basic security settings i use are: 1) mac address filtering 2) change the routers default name and password 3) disable ssid 4) enable either wep or wpa depending on your proximity to other wifi networks or neighbors will determine how many of these options you use.
